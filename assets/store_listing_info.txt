Nimbus Digital Weather Face


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 8000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Nimbus Digital is a premium, data-driven, weather-first watch face for your Garmin smartwatch. Designed as a clean and high-density information dashboard, it provides everything you need for your day at a single glance, without cluttered configurations.

Key Features:
- Large, high-visibility digital clock featuring a split-color layout (hour in accent theme, minutes in white/silver).
- Prominent live weather module featuring real-time temperature, daily high/low forecast, current conditions, and secondary weather metrics (precipitation %, relative humidity, wind speed).
- Clean and scalable vector-drawn procedural weather icons (Clear, Cloudy, Rainy, Snowy, Stormy, Windy, Foggy, Mixed).
- Fully integrated 5-day daily forecast rows showing daily conditions and temperature ranges.
- Active sensor readings: steps count and real-time heart rate (BPM) tracking.
- 4-column activity footer: Calories burned, Distance traveled, Floors climbed, and Active minutes.
- Comprehensive status header: Battery (with color-coded battery icon), solar intensity charging rate, Bluetooth connection state, notifications/DND, and active alarms.
- Clean and efficient Always-On-Display (AOD) sleep layout with pixel-shifting burn-in protection.
- Customization: 8 premium color themes (Midnight Gold, Rose Gold, Arctic Teal, Ember Orange, Forest Green, Lavender Mist, Pearl Silver, Solar Amber) and a seconds hand display toggle.
