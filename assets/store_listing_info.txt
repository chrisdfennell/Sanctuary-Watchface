Sanctuary — Diablo-Inspired Watch Face

(Alternates: "Sanctuary — Diablo Life & Mana Globes" /
 "Sanctuary: Life & Mana Resource Globes")


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 4000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Sanctuary is a dark, gothic watch face inspired by the iconic health and mana orbs of classic action-RPGs. Two glowing resource globes flank a bold central clock set in the Exocet typeface, turning your wrist stats into a tactical HUD.

GLOWING RESOURCE GLOBES
• LIFE (left, crimson): your Body Battery, filling from the bottom 0–100. Watch your energy drain and recharge like a health orb.
• MANA (right, sapphire): your device battery, 0–100%. One glance tells you how much charge remains.
Each globe sits in an ornate riveted metal bezel, with a liquid fill, a bright surface line, and a glass highlight.

AT A GLANCE
• Large Exocet time (12/24h follows your watch setting)
• Optional date line with an ornamental divider
• A steps "XP" bar across the bottom that fills toward your daily step goal

BUILT FOR YOUR DISPLAY
Sanctuary adapts to your panel:
• AMOLED (Fenix 8 / tactix 8 / quatix 8 and Fenix 8 Pro): smooth gradient globes, soft glows, molten cores, and a full Diablo-inspired background.
• MIP / Solar (tactix 8 Solar, Fenix 8 Solar): clean, high-contrast flat globes and a deep-black background tuned for sunlight legibility.
Everything is laid out relative to your screen, so it looks right on every supported case size.

ALWAYS-ON FRIENDLY
On AMOLED, the always-on display switches to a burn-in-safe variant — dim time, thin globe rings, and shifting pixels. MIP panels always show the full face.

SETTINGS
• Show Date — toggle the date line
• Step Goal Override — set a custom steps target for the XP bar, or use your watch's own goal

PRIVACY
Sanctuary uses only the SensorHistory permission to read Body Battery. It does not collect or transmit any data off your watch.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
tactix 8 (AMOLED & Solar), Fenix 8 (43/47/51mm AMOLED), Fenix 8 Solar, Fenix 8 Pro, quatix 8.

Equip Sanctuary, and keep watch.

Version 1.0.1 — Solar & MIP support

Sanctuary now runs beautifully on memory-in-pixel (MIP) displays like the tactix 8 Solar and Fenix 8 Solar — not just AMOLED.

• Per-display fonts: the Exocet typeface is now sized for every panel (454, 416, 280, and 260 px), so the time, date, and labels fit cleanly on the smaller Solar screens.
• Clean flat globes on MIP: the LIFE and MANA globes use a crisp two-tone fill tuned for MIP's palette — no gradient banding — while AMOLED keeps the smooth liquid gradient and full background art.
• Full face in always-on on MIP: these panels stay in low power but still show the whole display, so Sanctuary now renders the complete face there. (The dimmed always-on variant is reserved for AMOLED burn-in protection.)
• Solid-black background on MIP for maximum contrast and sunlight legibility.
• More robust rendering: the screen always clears to black first, preventing stale-pixel artifacts.

On a Fenix 8 or tactix 8 AMOLED? Your experience is unchanged — rich gradients, glows, and the gothic background remain.

Thanks for wearing Sanctuary!
